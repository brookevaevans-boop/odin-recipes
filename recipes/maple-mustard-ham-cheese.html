Maple Mustard Ham and Cheese Sandwich

This recipe was born out of necessity. Its eponymous sauce is a marriage of the classic honey mustard with a twist that will remind you of a succulent maple-glazed ham.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 thick-cut slices of ham
  • 2 slices of your favorite cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Maple syrup
  • Whole grain mustard
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat
  2. In a small bowl, mix 2 parts mustard and 1 part maple syrup
  3. Spread mustard mixture on one slice of bread and and mayonnaise on the other
  4. Stack ham and cheese slices on top of the maple mustard and place other slice on top, mayonnaise side down
  5. Add a bit of butter to your pan to melt
  6. Place sandwich in pan for 2-3 minutes, or until bread is toasted to your liking. Flip and repeat
  7. Slice your sandwich on the diagonal and serve with a crunchy pickle, and your favorite soup or potato chips
