Cottage Pie

Description

A warm, delicious cottage pie perfect for hungry bellies during the cold autumn and winter seasons.

with just a few simple ingredients, your entire family will happily scarf down their plates. Don't be surprised when this is highly requested later!

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 2 large celery stalks
  • 1 white onion
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 80g butter
  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • worcestershire sauce to taste
  • parsley, sage, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and boil potatoes for 20 minutes or until fork tender
  3. While potatoes boil, dice carrots, celery, and onion to make your mirepoix
  4. Drain potatoes, and melt butter in the pot the potatoes were in. Add your milk to the butter along with your potatoes and mash them until smooth. add salt to taste. Set aside
  5. Heat a large pan on medium-high heat. Add some cooking oil along with your mirepoix. Cook, stirring occasionally until onions are translucent
  6. Add ground beef to the mirepoix. cook until browned
  7. Add minced garlic and herbs, and cook for about 1 minute or until fragrant
  8. Add Tomato paste, worcestershire sauce, and 3/4 cups of water. lower heat and simmer until thickened
  9. In a baking dish, evenly spread your ground beef mixture. Evenly spread your mashed potatoes on top of this layer. Use a piping bag for easier distribution
  10. Place baking dish in oven and bake for 30 minutes or until the tops of the potatoes become golden brown
  11. let cool for 15-20 minutes before serving
