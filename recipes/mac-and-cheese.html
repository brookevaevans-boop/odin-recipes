Mac and Cheese


Description

The ultimate comfort food. This warm and gooey dish will fill your belly and soul alike.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk
  • 80g butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 box (4 servings) elbow pasta
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 2/3 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup gouda cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup provolone cheese
  • Green onions (for garnish)
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta per box instructions
  2. While pasta is draining, in the same pot, melt butter over low heat
  3. Add flour and mix until smooth
  4. Gradually add milk while stirring constantly until fully incorporated
  5. Bring up heat and allow the mixture to simmer until thickened
  6. Lower heat and add cheese, stirring until well combined
  7. Add seasonings to taste
  8. Stir in cooked pasta and serve immediately, adding additional parmesan and green onions for garnish
